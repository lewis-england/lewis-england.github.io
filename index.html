Hitcham Bell and Organ Project

A digital timeline - please scroll down

October 2019

Organ dismantled & restoration of pipes begun

November 2019 - 1

The bells were lowered

November 2019 - 2

A night time watch ensured their safety.

November 2019 - 3

The old belfry was cleared out.

November 2019 - 4

The bells were moved for restoration.

June 2020

The bell cast is made.

August -> September 2020 - 1

The old belfry is further cleared out and floor is built to make a silences chamber.

August -> September 2020 - 2

Items found during the clearing of the belfry. These have now been donated to the Museum of East Anglian Life.

September 2020

Pockets for the new bell frame were cut and drains dug.

October 2020 - 1

The bell frame frame arrives and installation begins (architect's plan depicted)

October 2020 - 2

The bells were raised again.

November 2020 - 1

The MEAL Exhibition opened via BBC radio Suffolk. Prof. Henslow's Botanical Diagrams are depicted.

November 2020 - 2

The MEAL Exhibition opened via BBC radio Suffolk. An assorted arrangement of objects related to Prof. Henslow's work are depicted .

November 2020 - 3

The MEAL Exhibition opened via BBC radio Suffolk. Some members of the Hicham Project attend the Museum of East Anglian Life.

January 2021

CCTV is fitted, giving the bell ringers a live view from the ringing floor of the bells being rung. Building work continues.

February 2021

The Henslow display in church is completed and put up for display.

March 2021

The building is completed
